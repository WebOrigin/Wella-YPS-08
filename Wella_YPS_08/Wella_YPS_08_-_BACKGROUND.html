The Wella YPS Awards are designed to recognise and reward the talent, creativity, individuality and flair of New Zealand’s up and coming young stylists.  To compete in the Wella YPS Awards, stylists must be 22 years of age or under as of 31st March 2008. The awards are unique to the industry as this is the only event that judges competitors on all aspects of their workmanship, from how they hold their scissors to how they section the models’ hair – the judging panel are looking for the complete package in the winners. Each competition is a live event, where stylists have the opportunity to produce a cut, style and finish, in line with fashion trends of today and into the future. 


Wella Professionals has undertaken the Wella YPS Awards for the past 16 years to help build the careers of hairdressing's emerging talent through national and international exposure and competition experience while assisting in aligning and building knowledge for young hairdressers on the trends in the hair and fashion industries.


The competition consists of twelve regional heats throughout the country during March and April, which will determine up to 20 winners who will compete in the National Final held in Auckland in June. The build up to the final event runs over 2 days of strenuous activity where the contestants and their models work around the clock to create the look they desire.
