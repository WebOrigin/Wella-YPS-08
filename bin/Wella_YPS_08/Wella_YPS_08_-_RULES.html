Q: What is the age limit for entering the Wella YPS Awards?

A: You must be 22 years old or younger at 31st March 2008.


Q: When selecting my model can I use a male?

A: Yes you can use a male, when selecting your model make sure you think about model suitability to your chosen cut, hair texture and confidence of your model.


Q: Who will be judging at my regional competition?

A: The 2008 Regional Judges are part of a six person panel, for a full listing of judges please refer to the Judging section of this website. Two members of this panel will complete YPS Regional judging for one week, the following week two different judges from the panel will judge. Also introduced in 2008 is the use of Trainee Judges who are past Wella YPS Regional Winners who are no longer able to enter the competition due to the age restriction.


Q: When entering the competition floor should my model’s hair be wet or dry?

A: Your model should enter the floor with dry hair. You will be allocated time to wet down your model’s hair before the competition begins. When the competition begins all models will have wet hair.


Q: When I cut my model’s hair, can I do a dry cut?

A: Yes you can do a dry haircut, but remember that judges will also be marking you on your blowdrying ability, so you do need to demonstrate your skills in this.


Q: How do you work out how many Regional Winners there will be at each competition?

A: The number of Regional Winners will reflect the number of competitors: Up to 20 competitors = 1 winner; 20 – 40 = 2 winners; 41 – 60 = 3 winners, etc.


Q: Are my family and salon colleagues able to come and support me at the regional competitions?

A: Yes, anyone can attend the Wella YPS Regional competitions, there will be a door charge of $10 per person on entry.
